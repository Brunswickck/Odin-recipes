Peanut Butter and Marmite Toastie

Description

Simply put this is the best snack in the world. It ticks most of the important boxes that a snack should: Vegan, Quick & Extra Filling

(For a sweeter version of this snack, you can replace marmite with mashed banana!)

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Marmite

Instructions

  1. Toast two slices of bread
  2. Once toasted add peanut butter to one slice, and marmite to the other.
  3. Put the two pieces of toast together, so the marmite and peanut butter are facing towards each other (very important!)
