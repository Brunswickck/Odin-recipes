Rice and beans

Description

Simply put this is the best meal in the world. It ticks every important box that a meal should: Vegan, Tasty, Filling & Healthy

Ingredients

  • Microwave rice
  • Beans
  • Any vegetables
  • Ras el hanout

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a big pan on med-high heat
  2. Add the beans and the veg to the pan
  3. Add salt and ras el hanout to taste
  4. Once everything is nearly cooked then add the rice to the pan
  5. Cook for a few more minutes then serve
